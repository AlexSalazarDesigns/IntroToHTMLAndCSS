What is a 'best practice?'

"A method or technique that has consistently shown results superior to those achieved with other means, and that is used as a benchmark." (Wikipedia)

Communities such as W3C.org develop and propose best practices to the entire world. Additionally, companies may develop their own specific best practices that apply to their branding or coding standards

Best Practice Examples

  • Every open tag should have a matching close tag
  • when writing, don't mix your cases - all caps, all lower case, but do not mix them
  • Move formatting (font color, size, font style) to CSS
  • Validate often - see how the code behaves in multiple browser and mobile formats.
